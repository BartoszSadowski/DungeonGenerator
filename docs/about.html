About

Good to see you!

Welcome to my Dungeon Generator. In order to create new dungeon go to Dungeon Generator page and click 'New Dungeon' button, or just press 'R' key on your keyboard.

In Config page you will be able to configure your dungeon. Scale property is responsible for telling the generator how big pieces, from which the dungeon is being build from, should be. Rooms height and width will give generator a hint as to how big rooms can get. Despite how big values you set, expect some smaller rooms to fill the gaps. Denseness will tell dungeon how big gaps should appear.

Danger and Loot configuration are responsible for telling the generator how likely it is to find enemy, or treasure. Safer dungeons will have on average fewer enemies than dangerous once. Generous dungeons should have more loot than stingy once.

Have fun using it during your adventures ;)
